Dalriada SS (No. 247)

Last update - 26-Jun-2006
Vessel Type: Steam Collier Location: Co. Antrim
Date of Loss: 26th April 1910 Place: The Maidens
Cause of Loss: Ran aground Boat Dive from: Ballylumford
Charted Depth: 15m Irish O.S. Map: Discovery Series No. 9
Height of Wreck: 3m Admiralty Chart No: 2198
Hull Material: Iron Latitude (GPS): 54° 56' 59.76" North
Type of Seabed: Rocks Longitude (GPS): 05° 44' 28.74" West
Average Visibility: 5 - 10m Diving Experience: Novice
Historical & Diving Notes
  • Built in 1881 by J. Fullerton & Co., Paisley; grossed 324 tons and measured 46.67 x 7.01 x 3.17m.
  • Powered by a 2 cylinder compound steam engine and owned by W. Robertson, Glasgow.
  • Ran aground on Allen Rock, North of the Maidens while carrying coal from Ayr to Larne.
  • Lies on the East side of Allen Rock in 5 - 15m of water; launch from Ballylumford Harbour.
  • Recommended dive time is 30 mins. before High or Low Water Belfast.
