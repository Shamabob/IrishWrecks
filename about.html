About the Author

Randal Armstrong

Irishwrecksonline - on the internet since 1997

This award winning site is the result of many years of gathering and collating information on the dived wrecks around the whole of the Irish coast. Information held on each wreck site is in a "potted" form, as most divers only require the bare bones with regards to historical details.

Special thanks to Dr. Edward J. Bourke, author Ian Wilson, and Michael Montgomery for their contributions to this database.

"Look but not touch, so that others coming behind you can marvel at the treasures beneath our waves."
