Last update - 07-Jun-2020
Vessel Type: Irish Motor Trawler Location: Co. Donegal
Date of Loss: 4th November 2008 Place: St. John's Point/Drumanoo Head
Cause of Loss: Swamped and sank Boat Dive from: Killybegs or Teelin
Charted Depth: 30m - 35m Irish O.S. Map: Discovery Series No. 10

Average Visibility: 10 - 15m Diving Experience: Experienced
Historical & Diving Notes
  • Built in 1981, this Sligo registered seine net trawler grossed 34 tons and was approx. 51ft long.
  • Powered by a single diesel driven screw.
  • Swamped and sank at around 6.25am while pair fishing for herring and shifting the catch to the MFV Rachel D.
  • Owner/skipper Hughie Moore and his crew were successfully rescued by the MFV Rachel D.
  • Diving: The wreck lies 0.75 miles South of Drumanoo Head on its starboard side at around 25m depth.
  • WARNING: Be cautious of potential snagged nets remaining on the wreck.
