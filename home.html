About Shipwreck Explorers

Who We Are

Shipwreck Explorers is a community dedicated to discovering, documenting, and discussing historic shipwreck sites around the world. We connect divers, historians, and ocean explorers who share a passion for maritime history.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve maritime heritage, share underwater discoveries, and inspire responsible diving exploration through community collaboration and knowledge exchange.
